# Code of Conduct

GameWeave contributors must communicate professionally and focus discussion on technical work.

- Be respectful of different experience levels and viewpoints.
- Critique code and ideas, not people.
- Do not harass, threaten, discriminate, or publish another person's private information.
- Do not submit malicious code, credentials, or material you do not have permission to share.

Maintainers may edit or remove content and restrict participation when these rules are violated. Report serious or private conduct concerns through the repository's private vulnerability reporting channel so they are not exposed in a public issue.
